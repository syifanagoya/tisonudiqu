Pom Klementieff Net Worth 2024

She is best known for her role as Mantis in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, which has significantly contributed to her net worth. As of 2024, her net worth is estimated to be quite impressive, thanks to her successful acting career. This article will delve into the details of Pom Klementieff’s net worth in 2024, her sources of wealth, and her journey in the entertainment industry.

Early Life and Career Beginnings

Pom Klementieff was born in Quebec City, Canada, to a Korean mother and French-Russian father. She spent her early years traveling around the world due to her father’s job as a consul. After his death, she settled in France with her mother and brother. Her acting career began in France, where she attended the Cours Florent drama school and landed her first acting role in the French independent film “Après lui” in 2007.

Rise to Stardom

Klementieff’s breakthrough came in 2013 when she starred in Spike Lee’s “Oldboy.” Her performance in the film caught the attention of Hollywood, leading to roles in films like “Hacker’s Game” and “Ingrid Goes West.” However, her most notable role came in 2017 when she was cast as Mantis in “Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2,” a part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe. This role significantly boosted her fame and net worth.

Marvel Cinematic Universe and Beyond

After her debut in “Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2,” Klementieff reprised her role as Mantis in “Avengers: Infinity War,” “Avengers: Endgame,” and “Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3.” These films were box office hits, contributing significantly to her net worth. Apart from the Marvel Cinematic Universe, Klementieff has also appeared in other films like “Uncut Gems” and “Mission: Impossible – Fallout,” further increasing her wealth.

Net Worth and Earnings

As of 2024, Pom Klementieff’s net worth is estimated to be around $4 million. The majority of her wealth comes from her acting career, particularly her roles in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Her earnings also include her paychecks from other film and television roles, as well as endorsements and sponsorships.

Real Estate Investments

Like many celebrities, Klementieff has invested a portion of her wealth in real estate. She owns a luxurious home in Los Angeles, California, which adds to her net worth.

Endorsements and Sponsorships

Klementieff has also earned money from endorsements and sponsorships. She has worked with several brands and has appeared in advertising campaigns, which have contributed to her net worth.

Philanthropy

Despite her wealth, Klementieff is known for her philanthropic efforts. She has supported various charities and causes, demonstrating her commitment to giving back to the community.

Future Projects

Klementieff’s net worth is expected to increase in the future as she continues to land roles in high-profile films. She is set to appear in upcoming films like “Mission: Impossible 7” and “Mission: Impossible 8,” which will likely add to her wealth.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Pom Klementieff’s net worth in 2024 is a testament to her successful acting career.
