Haley Reeves Barbour (born October 22, 1947) is an American politician who served as the 62nd Governor of the state of Mississippi from 2004 until 2012. She is a member of the Republican Party. When Hurricane Katrina struck the state of Mississippi in August 2005, he was thrust into the national limelight. Prior to being elected to public office, Barbour was a well-known lobbyist and co-founder of the Washington lobbying company BGR Group, which he founded with his brother.
