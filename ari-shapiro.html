Biography

Ari Shapiro was born in Fargo, ND on September 30, 1978. Popular radio journalist who became one of four hosts on NPR’s flagship news series All Things Considered in 2015. He was previously a White House correspondent and has received numerous honors including the American Bar Association’s Silver Gavel Award and the Daniel Schorr Journalism Prize. He earned his fame as a host on NPR like Carl Kasell and Terry Gross. He graduated magna cum laude from Yale University with an English degree in 2000.
