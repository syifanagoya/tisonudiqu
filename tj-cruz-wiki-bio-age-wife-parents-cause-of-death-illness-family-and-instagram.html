TJ Cruz Biography – TJ Cruz Cruz Wiki

TJ Cruz born Tristan Jedidiah, is the eldest son of actor Tirso Cruz III and Lyn Ynchausti. He was born on October 22, 1981. Teejay graduated with a degree in International Studies from De La Salle University.

Teejay was part of the show Ang TV in the ’90s. He also co-starred in films Eskapo and Rollerboys, which were both released in 1995. He then founded an organization called Spike for Hope, a volleyball tournament that raises funds for children with severe illnesses.
