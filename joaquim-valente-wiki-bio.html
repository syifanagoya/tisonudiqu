Joaquim Valente Family

The Valente family is a fighting family with a history in jiujitsu. Joaquim Valente Jiu- Jitsu

The jjutsu legacy was passed down to the Valente brothers from birth. Before they could walk, Pedro, Gui, and Joaquim started studying jiu-jitsu with their father. Grandmaster Hélio Gracie began giving Pedro, Gui, and Joaquim special training when they were just two years old. The Valente brothers received daily instruction from Hélio, Royler, and Rolker Gracie at the Gracie Academy in Rio de Janeiro while they were growing up. Pedro Sr. always desired for his sons to learn the full fighting system of jiu-jitsu.

He understood that training techniques had evolved since he had been trained in the 1950s. Group classes nearly entirely concentrated on ground grappling tactics to prepare pupils for contests. The Valente brothers began studying the full jjutsu system in regular private lessons with Grandmaster Helio, which helped to address this problem, but they also began training with top-tier boxing and jiu-jitsu practitioners at a young age.
