Revisiting ANTM

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, April 13, 2024

Before I begin: Yes, I am ashamed of myself for watching this.

Now: Amazon Prime rolled over automatically from the new 'Making the Cut' episode to the first-ever episode of ANTM and I did not turn it off. Wow, is it a time capsule.

All the girls are skinny, of course. One is considered plus size because she has thighs. Tyra laments that the girl's top half doesn't match her bottom half, being thinner, and thus making it harder to fit her into either 'normal' or 'plus size' category, and yet all the judges basically say she is too fat to be a model. She's not fat at all, though.

One contestant is a HS graduate who is positioned as a 'scientist' and 'an academic' becsuse she applied to medical schools. She has not yet gone to college because 'I can always do that, but I can't always be 20.' She describes herself as a militant atheist and she is being harassed by three Christian girls who made her read from the Bible. She is emaciated and the other models and some of the judges have questioned whether she has an eating disorder, even as all the judges, Miss J., the runway coach, all say she has the most potential because "she's the only one with a body for clothes."

The militant atheist/academic who has never been to college is a smoker, and she and another girl smoke inside. That was shocking to me. I can't imagine that happening now.

Janice Dickenson is a horrible human being but still entertaining.

Kimora Lee Simmons was an ANTM judge during the first season?!

It's kind of bizarre to see these starved-looking '90s versions of models call one another fat in these days of fat asses and fake boobs and duck lips.

It's a little surprising that the cast is as ethnically diverse as TV shows today are. Notably missing is an Asian American girl (one may be of Indian descent but I'm not sure) but all the other boxes are checked.

One late addition to the cast is a Latina and she has been chastised several times already for looking too sultry because that is how her face looked. Miss J dressed her in a suede fringed crop top and told her she has the perfect "sexy Pocahontas look."

I iterate that I am ashamed of myself for watching this. And I will keep watching.
